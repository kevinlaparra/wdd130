Purpose

The purpose of this site is to create a portfolio of my experience, skills, and hobbies. I will include previous jobs, skills i have gained from courses, hobbies i enjoy, and more details about myself to create a more personal portfolio.

Audience

My intended audience is potential future employees. I intend to show them my portfolio to not only show them who i am and what i can do but to also show how i have put what i learned to use.

Paragraph Topic: Introduction

Welcome to my portfolio website! Here, you will discover who I am, explore my professional journey, and gain deeper insights into my skills and passions. Whether you are a potential client, employer, or simply curious about my work, this website aims to provide you with a comprehensive overview of my background and expertise. Visitors to my site will expect to find answers to questions such as who I am, my professional strengths, experiences, qualifications, and whether I can meet their specific needs or requirements. They also want to know about my past projects, outcomes, personal interests, and how to get in touch with me.

Paragraph Topic: Activities on the Website

Visitors will engage in various activities while exploring my portfolio website, including navigating through different sections to gain a holistic understanding of my background and skills. They will read about specific projects or experiences that align with their interests, review testimonials or recommendations, explore my educational background and achievements, and discover my personal interests and hobbies to establish a deeper connection. Visitors will come to my site instead of others because of its clear organization, visually appealing design, and comprehensive content. The combination of a well-presented biography, detailed work experience, personal insights, and visual examples of my work sets my portfolio website apart. By providing a user-friendly interface and valuable information, I aim to make visitors feel confident in their decision to choose me for their projects or collaborations.

Paragraph Topic: Necessary Information

To provide visitors with a comprehensive experience, my site will include a detailed biography highlighting my professional journey, expertise, and key achievements. It will also feature an organized section dedicated to my work experience, including job titles, responsibilities, and notable projects. Additionally, it will showcase my educational background, degrees earned, and any relevant certifications. Visual examples and case studies of my best work will be provided to showcase my skills and capabilities. Testimonials or recommendations from satisfied clients or colleagues will be included, along with a section that delves into my personal interests, hobbies, and passions. Contact information, including email address, phone number, and links to professional social media profiles, will be readily available.
